Logical Connectives: NOT, AND, OR.

  • Negation.
    NOT. Notation: \(\neg p\text{,}\) read as “not \(p\text{.}\)”
  • Conjunction.
    AND. Notation: \(p\wedge q\text{,}\) read as “\(p\) and \(q\text{.}\)”
  • Disjunction.
    OR. Notation: \(p\vee q\text{,}\) read as “\(p\) or \(q\text{.}\)”
